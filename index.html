About me

Hello! I'm Kristen, a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Diversity Lab at the University of Arizona. I graduated with my PhD in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology from the Univeristy of Idaho, where I studied in the Harmon Lab. I love writing software to help explain patterns of evolution and biodiversity, especially when those patterns are observed across island systems and within evolutionary video games.
